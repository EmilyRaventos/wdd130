Why Rafting?

What are benefits of white water rafting?

White water rafting offers a variety of benefits! It can be an intense cardio work out and an adrenaline filled experience. It can also be a great way to see beautiful sites with friends and family. And don't forget it's fun!

How many people fit on a raft?

Our rafts vary in size to help you find the best fit for your group! We have a large raft that can hold 12 people, the regular raft will hold eight, and the smaller raft holds four.

Preparation

What should I wear

Be comfortable! Swimwear and comfortable footwear are recommended. Other items you might want to bring include: hats, sunglasses, and sunscreen.

What should I bring?

We will provide the necessary supplies for you to participate in your rafting adventure! You're welcome to bring water, snacks, cameras and weather appropriate clothing in addition to what we provide.

How long will I be rafting?

The duration of the trip depends completely on the river you choose. Contact us to find out details of a specific river.

Safety

Do I need to have experience rafting?

First time rafters are welcome! We have many different locations for rafters of all experience levels.

Do I need to know how to swim?

No, you don't need to know how to swim to enjoy white water rafting. While knowing how to swim may be helpful, you will be wearing life preservers and have an experienced guide with you to minimize any risks!
