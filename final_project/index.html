about me

Hello! I'm Kylee Raventos--an artist, writer, and content creator from Utah, USA. In 2020, I posted my first ever digital artwork on Instagram, kicking off my small freelancing career and digital art journey. Since then, I've been commissioned to draw dozens of original characters and my small art account has gained over 5k wonderful followers.
I'm an avid lover of Star Wars, the Lord of the Rings, Avatar, and Harry Potter, as well as my personal collection of books (which are currently crammed onto every spare shelf in my room). My favorite authors are R.F. Kuang, Maggie Stiefvater, Abbi Geni, and Leigh Bardugo. I'm currently writing a fantasy duology codenamed "Project B" which follows a young bookkeeper determined to discover the secrets of a magical bookstore and keep it out of the hands of monsters.
I'll graduate with my Bachelor's degree in Political Science in the fall of 2024. Until and after then, I plan to continue posting my work, publish "Project B", and begin printing and selling my art from home.
